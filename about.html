About Page

This project website is an assignment for IS601 - Spring 2021. It outlines the history of the internet, from its inception to what we know about it today. It is imperative that we know how the internet began, as it gives all of us important insight into what it took to get to where we are today.

The website will take the user from the invention of the internet, to the beginning of the browser wars and the evolution of the internet, and to the important figures that were vital to the creation and growth of the internet.
